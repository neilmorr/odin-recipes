Steak

Description

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic butter that makes it taste like a steakhouse quality meal.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Sear both sides of the steak
  2. Cook on one side for 7 1/2 minutes at 350 degrees
  3. Flip and cook other side for 4 minutes
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
